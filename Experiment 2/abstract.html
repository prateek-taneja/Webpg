Abstract

A website where students can visit to build a good resume and to get a head start in their career by gaining the awareness about companies job descriptons, eligibility criterias, applicattion date and deadlines etc. One of the biggest trials for many job-seekers is creating the perfect resume. You can find a professional to do it for you, or you can use a template, but if you are a victim of the do-it-yourself attitude (like most of us in IT) then you need to know how to include your IT skills in a clean and readable format.
